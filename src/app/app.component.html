Secret Santa Generator

In my and my wife's families we always randomize who is gonna buy a christmas gift to whom.
Not does this only save alot of money but it also ease up on the stress that christmas is highly associated with.

Although all of the generators available online (atleast from what I've found) is way too complex, requires emails and yadayada.
So therefore I decided to make this simple generator.

Simply type in the names of the participants to the left, press the "randomize"-button and it's all done!
