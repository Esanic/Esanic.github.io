Secret Santa generator

In my family we always randomize who is gonna buy a christmas gift to whom.

All of the generators available online (atleast from what I've found) is way too complex and requires emails and yadayada.
So therefore I decided to make this simple generator.

Simply type in the names of the participants to the left, press the "randomize"-button and it's all done!
